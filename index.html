  • Ross Ginn | UI/UX Designer, Web Developer

    My journey in the world of design started at Western Kentucky University graphic design program. There I learned the various strategies of visual design and further honed them with my internship at the Department of Publications. Upon graduation I needed something to set me apart from my peers. I received some great advice from someone in the field to educate myself in web design and development. This gave me a goal to shoot for. Soon after, I designed and developed my first site. This gave me a new set of tools to add to my bag. Soon after I was inspired by a fellow WKU graduate to pursue a career in UX design. To further my education in UX I enrolled in Bloc's UI/UX design apprenticeship. My mentor guided me through my projects and taught me the fundamentals of intuitive design. Bloc also taught me various research methods that allow me to narrow down who the target user is.
